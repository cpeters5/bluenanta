Welcome to McGivens
McGivens is a new program with the mission to provide a venue for Givens' artist residents and local Artists in Asheville to show their artworks to the world.
At McGivens, we believe that art should be accessible to all. That's why we're offering a unique opportunity for resident artists to exhibit their creations to a wider audience. Whether you're a painter, sculptor, photographer, or any other form of artist, our platform welcomes you with open arms.
Here's what you can expect from McGivens
  • Free Artwork Posting: Share your masterpieces without any upfront charges! Our platform allows you to upload high-quality pictures of your artworks at absolutely no cost. This means you can showcase your talent and build your artistic portfolio without any financial burden.
  • Sales Made Simple (in progress): We understand that artists deserve recognition and financial support for their hard work. That's why we plan to develop a seamless sales system on our website. When a piece of your artwork finds a loving home, we'll facilitate the transaction and collect a small fee as a commission. This fee will not only help us maintain the website but also enable us to continuously improve and expand our services to benefit the entire artist community.
  • Promotional Boost: We're committed to promoting your talent to a wider audience. Through our website, social media channels, and various marketing initiatives, we'll ensure that your artworks gain the visibility they deserve. Get ready to attract art enthusiasts, collectors, and potential buyers who appreciate your unique creations.
