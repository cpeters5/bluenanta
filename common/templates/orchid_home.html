BlueNanta is a new project under the OrchidRoots, a non-profit organization. The initial mission of OrchdRoots is to develop an educational tool that provides visual identification and comparison of orchids. The goal of the new BlueNanta project is to expand the coverage of OrchidRoots to other plant families.
