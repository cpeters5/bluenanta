You are invited to submit an advertisement about your plant related business, a meeting announcement, or anything else related to plants that you want the world to know about.

To submit your ad, email the following information to admin@orchidroots.org:

  • Who you are
  • your orchidroots account id
  • email address
  • Brief address (country, state, city)
  • The name of your business
  • Brief description
  • A thumbnail or a jpeg for your ad display.
  • (optional) url of your website
  • (optional) Type of plants involved (genus name, family, etc.) We will use this information to place your ad where it is most relevant
  • (optional) Start and end date. Higher priority will be given to ads requested for specific date range.

This ad is currently free. However, we encourage advertisers to donate to orchidRoots to support our work.
